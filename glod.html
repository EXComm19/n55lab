Product promise

A small oil bead focused on repair and calm.

The formula is organised as a water-rich serum and a single structured oleogel bead. Each part has a defined role rather than a decorative ingredient story.

Oleogel bead

Lab notebook

Development Story

“Glød was developed around a calm aqueous serum and one small oil bead for repair-support and soothing care.”

The development direction focuses on a small stable oil bead, soothing application, controlled redispersion and compatibility with moisturiser. It occupies the same routine slot as Rav Perle and Serum 3.

Frequently asked questions

Do I layer Glød with the other serums?+

No. Glød, Rav Perle and Serum 3 are alternatives within the same serum step.

When should I introduce it?+

After the cleanser and moisturiser starting pair feels comfortable.

Is it fragrance free?+

Yes. Glød contains no added fragrance.

Does it exfoliate?+

The current formulation direction is based on humectants, ectoin and a structured oleogel bead, not an exfoliating active routine.
