Lab notebook

Development Story

“RENS began with a simple formulation question: can a cleanser feel clean without feeling harsh?”

The development goal was not maximum foam. It was balance: a soft microfoam, a mild pH range, clean rinsing and a comfortable after-feel suitable for sensitive-skin routines.

During development, the surfactant system, clarity, odour profile, flow, foam structure and pump compatibility were adjusted through multiple trials. Each version was assessed not only for how it looked on day one, but how it behaved over time.

The final direction reflects the N°55 approach: restrained, barrier-conscious and tested for balance rather than excess.

Frequently asked questions

It is designed as the daily cleanser in the N°55 routine. Adjust frequency if your skin feels uncomfortable.

Rens is the first step, before toner, serum and moisturiser.

Is it fragrance free?+
