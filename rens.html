— 01 / The Formula

A cleanser calibrated to skin, not against it.

Most foaming cleansers are alkaline by design — efficient at lifting oil and debris, but indifferent to what they leave behind. Rens departs from that orthodoxy. Formulated at the skin's resting pH of 5.5, it is engineered around amino-acid surfactants drawn from coconut and sugar fermentation — a generation of cleansing agents that emulsify impurities without stripping the lipid barrier.

Glycerin and panthenol restore moisture in the same wash. Aloe leaf juice and betaine soothe. The result is a cleanser that closes — not breaks — the cycle of barrier integrity. Skin feels clean, but not bare. Comfortable, not tight.

Suited to daily use, morning and evening. Compatible with all subsequent steps of the N°55 ritual.

— 03 / The Ritual

Three motions, twice daily.

— Motion 01
Wet

Wet face with lukewarm — never hot — water. Avoid friction; the warmth is preparation, not treatment.

— Motion 02
Lather

Dispense a single pump into clean hands. Lather between palms, then apply in slow circular movements across the face and neck. Avoid the eye contour.

— Motion 03
Rinse

Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Press dry with a soft cloth. Proceed to N°55.02 Tonik while skin is still receptive.
