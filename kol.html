Frequently asked questions

Is Køl a daily step?+

No. It is an optional weekly treatment.

Where does it sit?+

Use after cleansing on a selected evening, then follow with moisturiser.

Is it fragrance free?+

Yes. Køl contains no added fragrance.

Should reactive skin use it immediately?+

Establish comfort with cleanser and moisturiser first, then introduce optional treatments gradually.
