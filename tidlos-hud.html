    Cleanse

    Begin with Rens on damp skin.

    Apply

    Build

    Add toner or one serum only after the starting pair feels comfortable.

Frequently asked questions

Why is it in the starting pair?+

Moisturiser gives sensitive skin a simple, consistent endpoint after cleansing.

Can it be used without serum?+

Yes. Serum is optional and can be introduced later.

Is it fragrance free?+

Yes. Tidløs Hud contains no added fragrance.

Where does sunscreen fit?+

Use sunscreen as the final morning step according to its label directions.
