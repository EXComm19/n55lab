Lab notebook

Development Story

“The development brief for Tonik was to replace the “active toner” mindset with a quieter role: restore hydration after cleansing and prepare skin for moisturiser.”

The formula direction prioritises a light mist, a mild pH target and a finish that does not leave sensitive skin feeling coated or overloaded.

Frequently asked questions

Is toner required from day one?+

No. Begin with cleanser and moisturiser, then introduce Tonik when skin feels comfortable.

Where does it sit?+

After cleanser and before serum or moisturiser.

Is it fragrance free?+

Yes. Tonik contains no added fragrance.

What if misting feels uncomfortable?+

Stop use and return to the starting pair. Seek professional advice for persistent irritation.
