Routine guidance

How to use

Introduce one new formula at a time. Final directions on the product label take precedence.

  1. 1

    Establish

    Begin with Rens and Tidløs Hud before adding a serum.

  2. 2

    Redistribute

    Gently mix the suspended phase before use if directed on the final label.

  3. 3

    Choose one

    Use Serum 3, Rav Perle or Glød in the serum step, then moisturise.

Frequently asked questions

Is Serum 3 the final name?+

No. It is the working name requested for the current product and may change before launch.

Do I layer all three serums?+

No. They are alternatives within the same routine step.

Is it fragrance free?+

The current batch formula contains no added fragrance. Final commercial INCI will be confirmed before launch.

When should sensitive skin introduce it?+

Only after the cleanser and moisturiser starting pair feels comfortable, and then gradually.
