Product promise

Brightening support with a distinct antioxidant phase.

The formula is organised as a water-rich serum and a structured bead. Each part has a defined role rather than a decorative ingredient story.

Humectant water phase

Glycerin and propylene glycol support a comfortable, water-rich application.

Niacinamide at 3%

Supports the brightening direction at a measured concentration.

Structured bead

Phenyl trimethicone, dimethicone, tocopherol and ubiquinone form the suspended oil-silicone phase.

Frequently asked questions

Do I layer Rav Perle with the other serums?+

Rav Perle, Glød and Serum 3 occupy the same serum step. Choose one according to your formula preference.

When should I introduce it?+

After the cleanser and moisturiser starting pair feels comfortable.

Is it fragrance free?+

Yes. Rav Perle contains no added fragrance.

Can I use it every day?+

Follow final label directions and introduce it gradually so you can observe tolerance.
