A routine that grows with tolerance

Start with two. Build only when skin is ready.

Sensitive skin rarely needs every formula at once. Establish comfort first, then add one product at a time so you can understand what your skin tolerates.

The starting protocol

Cleanser + moisturiser

Begin with Rens and Tidløs Hud. Keep the routine consistent and simple before introducing toner, serum or mask.

If you already use and tolerate sunscreen, continue it each morning. Patch test new formulas and seek professional advice for persistent irritation.
